# MailDraft Landing Page AI Builder Prompt

Use the following instructions to build a high-converting, premium landing page for **MailDraft**.

## 1. App Overview & Identity
- **Name**: MailDraft
- **Slogan**: Instant Access to Perfect Emails.
- **Value Proposition**: MailDraft is a smart template library that helps professionals, job seekers, and anyone who communicates via email to stop staring at blank drafts. It provides instant access to professionally-written emails for every workplace scenario.
- **Design Aesthetic**: Modern, premium, and "glassy" (Glassmorphism). Use smooth gradients, subtle micro-animations, and a clean, distraction-free layout. Prioritize a high-end feel with refined typography (e.g., Inter or Outfit).

## 2. Page Structure & Content

### Hero Section
- **Headline**: Stop Staring at Blank Email Drafts.
- **Sub-headline**: Get instant access to a curated library of professionally-written email templates for every workplace scenario. Copy, paste, and send in seconds.
- **Call to Action (CTA)**: "Get Early Access" (links to the lead generation form).
- **Visual**: A premium mock-up of the MailDraft interface showing a glassy template card being personalized.

### Benefits Section
- **Save Hours of Time**: No more agonizing over phrasing or tone.
- **Always Sound Professional**: Templates written by experts for high-stakes communication.
- **Smart Personalization**: Highlight the "smart placeholders" that make templates feel unique to the recipient.
- **Your Own Library**: Mention the ability to save and organize custom templates.

### Lead Generation Form
- **Objective**: Collect user information for a waitlist/beta.
- **Fields**:
  - Full Name (required)
  - Business Email (required, with validation)
- **Submit Button**: "Join the Waitlist"

### Survey / Discovery Section
- **Objective**: Understand user pain points.
- **Questions**:
  1. "What is your biggest challenge when writing professional emails?" (Text Area)
  2. "How many professional emails do you send in a typical day?" (Dropdown: 1-5, 6-15, 15+)
  3. "Which categories would you use most? (Meetings, Job Applications, Networking, Declining Requests)" (Multi-select)

### Footer
- Links to Socials, Privacy Policy (placeholders), and a "Built with MailDraft" badge.

## 3. Technical Requirements
- Build using **React** and **Tailwind CSS**.
- Ensure full responsiveness (mobile, tablet, desktop).
- Implement smooth scroll for navigation.
- Use a dark mode by default with a glassy UI theme.
- Supabase for storing the info collected
